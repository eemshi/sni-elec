We are an independent distributor of electronic parts since 2000, founded in New York, USA. In 2008, SNI Electronics moved to Georgia, USA where the main office is currently located.

We wholesale OEM excess parts, exporting mainly to South Korea and Japan. We have been a purchasing agent for Hanwha Thales (formerly known as Samsung Thales) since 2000, with our partner company Dain Semicom Inc. in Korea.

We are an authorized distributor of Kaydon and Timken for the Korean market, as well as Pico Electronics and Ledtronics. We also have a strong purchasing line for Altera, Analog Device, and Texas Instruments. We have 15 years of experience in specialized military parts.
